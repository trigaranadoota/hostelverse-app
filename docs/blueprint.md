# **App Name**: HostelVerse

## Core Features:

- Multi-Language Support: Offer language selection during onboarding and settings to support multiple languages.
- Hostel Discovery: Allow users to discover hostels through map and list views, with advanced filtering options such as gender, room sharing, and amenities.
- Detailed Hostel Profiles: Display hostel information, including address, contacts, amenities with ratings, fee structure, and verified badges.
- AI-Powered Image Verification: Use an AI tool to initially verify uploaded images of the hostel, rooms, and amenities, then show verification status with 'AI Verified' and 'Human Verified' badges after manual review.
- Ratings and Reviews System: Enable verified residents to post text reviews with optional image uploads, along with star ratings for food, cleanliness, management and safety.
- Visual Room Booking System: Implement a bus-style seat selection interface displaying hostel floor layouts with color-coded room availability and a booking option.
- Wishlist and Notifications: Notify users when wishlisted rooms become available or registration reopens.

## Style Guidelines:

- Overall app colors: grey and white.
- Buttons: purple.
- Room availability: red for maintenance.
- Room availability: green for available rooms.
- Room availability: yellow for occupied rooms.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font known for its neutral, objective, and readable qualities.
- Use simple, outlined icons to represent amenities and features, ensuring clarity and ease of understanding.
- Maintain a clean and modern interface, focusing on a mobile-first design approach to ensure accessibility and ease of use on smaller screens.
- The app should work well on both mobile and web.
- Incorporate subtle loading animations and transitions to provide visual feedback and enhance the user experience.
